Services

Learn more about our services and how we support residents in a safe environment.

Who is Eligible for Services?

Before applying check to ensure your eligible to register for our services:

  • Seniors 55+
  • Disabled (able to still manage daily living activities by themselves)
  • Re-entry citizens ages 55+
  • Transition from Homelessness
  • Veterans

Payments Accepted

We accept a variety of payment methods to accommodate the diverse needs of our residents. Our goal is to make housing accessible and affordable for everyone. Accepted payment methods include:

  • Social Security Income (SSI/SDI)
  • Private Pay
  • Government Voucher
  • Community Programs

Residency Requirements

Before applying residents must be able to manage daily living activities independently and agree to follow community guidelines designed to ensure a safe and respectful environment for all. Resident must meet the following requirements before applying:

  • Adult residents only
  • Ability to live independently without caregiving assistance
  • Ongoing stable income

What is Offered?

All services are provided to support residents in achieving stability, dignity, and independence, however they must be taken care of. Below you can find more information about our services.

Safe & Affordable Housing

Clean and secure housing options are provided to promote stability, comfort, and peace of mind. Residents are offered either private or shared living spaces that support personal independence while maintaining a safe and respectful environment.

Fully Furnished

All housing units are fully furnished with essential furniture and appliances to ensure comfort and convenience for residents. This includes beds, dressers, desks, chairs, and kitchen appliances to support a comfortable living environment.

Shared or Private Living

Residents are offered either private or shared living spaces that support personal independence while maintaining a safe and respectful environment.

Wi-Fi & Internet

High-speed internet access is available throughout all housing units, allowing residents to stay connected with loved ones, access online services, attend virtual appointments, pursue education or employment opportunities, and enjoy entertainment with reliable and consistent connectivity.

Security

Security cameras are installed in common and exterior areas to enhance safety and provide peace of mind for residents and families. Monitoring helps maintain a secure living environment while respecting personal privacy within individual living spaces.
