What is Comfort Care Housing Solutions?

Comfort Care Housing Solutions is a housing program dedicated to providing safe, affordable, and supportive housing for individuals in need. Our mission is to enhance the quality of life for our residents through compassionate care and community engagement.

Who We Serve

Learn more about the individuals we support and the services we provide. Inside the house, we offer a range of services tailored to meet the unique needs of each resident such as a bathroom, shared or private bedroom, and living room.

Discover Your Eligibility

Seniors

Seniors over the age of 55 can match within our program for housing support and independence.

Disability

Adults with physical disabilities who has their own independence can match within our program.

Veterans

Senior veterans who have served our country can match within our program for housing support and independence.

Re-entry Citizens

Individuals who are re-entering society after incarceration can match within our program for housing support and independence.

Our Services

Comfort Care Housing Solutions provides supportive, affordable housing designed to promote stability, dignity, and independence. Below you can find more information about our services.

Learn More

Safe & Affordable Housing

Clean and secure housing options are provided to promote stability, comfort, and peace of mind. Residents are offered either private or shared living spaces that support personal independence while maintaining a safe and respectful environment.

Fully Furnished

All housing units are fully furnished with essential furniture and appliances to ensure comfort and convenience for residents. This includes beds, dressers, desks, chairs, and kitchen appliances to support a comfortable living environment.

Shared or Private Living

Residents are offered either private or shared living spaces that support personal independence while maintaining a safe and respectful environment.

Wi-Fi & Internet

High-speed internet access is available throughout all housing units, allowing residents to stay connected with loved ones, access online services, attend virtual appointments, pursue education or employment opportunities, and enjoy entertainment with reliable and consistent connectivity.

Security

Security cameras are installed in common and exterior areas to enhance safety and provide peace of mind for residents and families. Monitoring helps maintain a secure living environment while respecting personal privacy within individual living spaces.
